NHS waiting list guide

Questions to ask when referred

  • What exactly have I been referred for?
  • Which hospital or team is the referral going to?
  • How will I be contacted?
  • What should I do if symptoms get worse?
  • Who can I contact if I hear nothing?

While waiting

Keep a symptom diary, attend tests, update your GP if things change, and keep letters or messages safe.

If symptoms change

If symptoms become urgent or you are unsure what to do, use NHS 111 or emergency services if appropriate.
