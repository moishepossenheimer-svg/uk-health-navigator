How to get a specialist referral in the UK

For non-emergency hospital treatment, you will usually start with a GP appointment. Explain your symptoms clearly and ask whether specialist referral is appropriate.

What to ask your GP

  • Do my symptoms need specialist review?
  • Which specialty is most relevant?
  • Are there tests we should do first?
  • Can I choose where I am referred?
  • What should I do if symptoms get worse while waiting?
