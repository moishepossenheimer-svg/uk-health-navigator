How to get a specialist referral in the UK

For non-emergency hospital treatment, you will usually need a GP referral. Your job is to explain the problem clearly and ask what the next step should be.

Before the GP appointment

  • Write your symptoms in date order.
  • List what you have already tried.
  • Bring test results, letters or photos if relevant.
  • Write how it affects daily life.

Questions to ask

  • Do my symptoms need specialist review?
  • Which specialty would be most relevant?
  • Should we do tests first?
  • Can I choose where I am referred?
  • What should I do if symptoms get worse while waiting?
