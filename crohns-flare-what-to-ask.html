Crohn's flare: what to ask

If Crohn's symptoms worsen, clear notes help your GP, IBD nurse or specialist team understand what has changed.

Track before contacting them

  • Bowel frequency and urgency
  • Blood or mucus
  • Pain location and severity
  • Temperature, weight, appetite and tiredness
  • Medicines, missed doses and recent infections

Questions to ask

  • Could this need blood tests or stool tests?
  • Should my medicine plan be reviewed?
  • Who is the best contact if it gets worse?
  • When should I use NHS 111 or urgent help?
