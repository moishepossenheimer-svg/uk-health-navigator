ADHD: where to ask for help

This page helps you prepare and navigate UK services. It is not a diagnosis or treatment plan.

Usual starting point

GP for referral pathway; specialist assessment services.

What to track before asking for help

Track attention, impulsivity, organisation, school/work impact, sleep and examples from daily life.

  • When symptoms started and whether they are getting better or worse
  • How often symptoms happen and what triggers them
  • How it affects school, work, sleep, eating, walking or daily life
  • Medicines, doses, missed doses, allergies and side effects
  • Any test results, letters or appointment dates you already have

Questions to ask

  • What is the most likely next step?
  • Do I need tests, monitoring, treatment review or referral?
  • What should I do if symptoms get worse while waiting?
  • Who should I contact for follow-up?
  • When should I expect results or a reply?
