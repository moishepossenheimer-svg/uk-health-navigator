GP vs NHS 111 vs A&E

Use GP for

Ongoing symptoms, long-term conditions, medication reviews, referral questions, fit notes and routine follow-up.

Use NHS 111 for

Medical help right now when you are not sure what to do, especially if your GP is closed or you cannot contact them.

Use A&E / 999 for

Possible life-threatening emergencies or immediate danger. Do not delay emergency help by searching online.

Use pharmacy for

Minor illnesses, common medicine questions and advice on whether you need another service.

Practical step-by-step

  1. Decide whether it is an emergency.
  2. If urgent but not sure, use NHS 111.
  3. If ongoing, write notes and contact GP.
  4. If minor, ask a pharmacist.
  5. Record what you were told and when to seek help again.
