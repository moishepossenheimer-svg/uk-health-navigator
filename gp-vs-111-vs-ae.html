Guide

GP vs NHS 111 vs A&E

Use GP

Ongoing symptoms, long-term conditions, referrals and routine medicine reviews.

Use NHS 111

When you need medical help now and you are not sure what to do.

Use A&E or 999

Use emergency care for life-threatening emergencies or immediate danger.

Use pharmacy

Minor illnesses, medicine advice and some emergency medicine supply routes.
