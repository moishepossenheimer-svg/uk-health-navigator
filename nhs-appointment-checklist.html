NHS appointment checklist

Bring or prepare

  • One-sentence summary of the main problem.
  • Timeline of symptoms.
  • Medicines, doses and allergies.
  • Previous letters, results or photos.
  • Three questions you want answered.

Before leaving

  • What is the plan?
  • When should I expect results?
  • Who do I contact if symptoms change?
  • Do I need a follow-up appointment?
