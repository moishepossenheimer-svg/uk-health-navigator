Blood test basics

Blood tests are interpreted by healthcare professionals in the context of symptoms, history, medicines and previous results. This page helps you prepare questions; it does not interpret results.

Questions to ask

  • Which results are outside the reference range?
  • Are these results new or similar to previous tests?
  • Could medicines or illness affect them?
  • Do I need repeat tests?
  • What symptoms should make me seek help sooner?
