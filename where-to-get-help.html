Where to get medical help in the UK

Emergency

If you think it is a life-threatening emergency, call 999 or go to A&E.

Need medical help now but unsure?

Use NHS 111 online or call 111. NHS 111 can direct people to the most suitable service.

Ongoing condition or referral

For ongoing symptoms or long-term conditions, your GP is usually the starting point. Hospital treatment usually needs a GP referral, except in emergencies.

Minor illness or medicine questions

A pharmacist can help with many minor illnesses and medicine questions.

Mental health

For mental health support, use NHS mental health services, GP support, or urgent help if needed.
