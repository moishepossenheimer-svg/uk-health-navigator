Crohn's & IBD help in the UK

Crohn's disease is a long-term inflammatory bowel condition. This page is for navigation and appointment preparation only.

Who to contact

  • GP: if symptoms are new, worse, or treatment is not helping.
  • NHS 111: if you need help now and are unsure what to do.
  • IBD nurse / gastroenterology team: if you already have a specialist team.
  • Crohn's & Colitis UK: charity information and support.

What to track before an appointment

  • Number of bowel movements per day
  • Blood or mucus
  • Pain location and severity
  • Weight change, appetite, tiredness
  • Current medicines and missed doses

Questions to ask

  • Do I need blood tests or stool tests?
  • Should my treatment plan be reviewed?
  • When should I contact the IBD nurse urgently?
  • Do I need dietitian support?
