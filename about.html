About UK Health Navigator

UK Health Navigator helps people understand where to ask for help in the UK and how to prepare for appointments.

It does not diagnose, treat, rank hospitals, recommend specific doctors, or replace professional care.

What this site is good for

  • Planning questions before appointments
  • Tracking symptoms
  • Understanding common NHS routes
  • Finding trusted official information

What this site is not for

  • Emergency decision-making
  • Diagnosis
  • Medication changes
  • Replacing a GP, pharmacist, NHS 111 or specialist team
